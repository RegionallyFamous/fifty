## Voice

Victorian apothecary: cream + rust, antique serif display, ornamental dividers.

- **Register**: workshop
- **Preferred motifs**: —

## Palette

Paper `#f5eed8` · ink `#1c1711` · accent `#8b2f1f`. Every other slug below is
derived from the brand palette; the alert family (success / warning / error
/ info) is retoned to sit on top of paper without hijacking the brand.

| Slug | Hex | Role |
|------|-----|------|
| `base` | `#f5eed8` | page background (paper) |
| `surface` | `#fdf8e8` | cards / panels |
| `subtle` | `#efe4c4` | gentle dividers |
| `contrast` | `#1c1711` | body text (ink) |
| `accent` | `#8b2f1f` | CTA + brand highlight |
| `accent-soft` | `#ead1b9` | tinted accent band |
| `primary` | `#1c1711` | resting brand action |
| `primary-hover` | `#4a2418` | hover state |

**Forbidden uses**:

- Accent applied to decorative elements (borders, dividers, icon fills) —
  accent is reserved for "this is the action".
- Off-palette colors anywhere outside the table above.
- Drop-shadows on anything except active form states.

## Typography

- `display` — Antique No (system-ui, Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif)
- `sans` — Crimson Pro (system-ui, Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif)

**Forbidden**:

- Display font on long-form body copy.
- All-caps body copy.
- Letter-spacing > 0.05em on body text.

## Composition

- **Hero**: type-led
- **Density**: tight
- **Ornament**: etched
- **Borders**: hard
- **Photography**: neutral

## Required signals

- All-caps display headline that dominates the hero
- Primary CTA reads as a slab, not a pill
- Etched / botanical line art accenting the hero or footer
- Borders are hard 1–2px contrast lines, not hairlines
- Maker / process / workshop vocabulary, batch + edition language
- Hero display heading may consume up to 50% of viewport height

## Forbidden signals

- Photographic hero with the headline reduced to caption size
- Drop-shadows or gradients used as decoration
- Default rounded button radius — buttons should be slabs

## Allowed exceptions

These document deliberate decisions in the shipped foundry theme.
The vision reviewer should treat them as intent, not regressions.

- Hero `display` heading on `home` may consume up to 50% of viewport
  height when `composition.hero` is `type-led` or `poster-led`.
- Buttons may be slabs (no border-radius) when `composition.borders` is
  `hard`.

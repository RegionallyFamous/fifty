One tile, one month,

Hand-painted Portuguese azulejos, drawn, cut, glazed and fired in our Lisbon oficina. The cobalt oxide is crushed by hand; the stencils are traced from sketchbooks older than the building; the kiln has been lit on Tuesdays since 1962.

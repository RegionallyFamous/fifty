## Voice

Bauhaus stationery press: circle + square + triangle hero icon, red + yellow + blue + cream, Universal-style geometric type.

- **Register**: editorial
- **Preferred motifs**: —

## Palette

Paper `#f3e9cc` · ink `#1f5191` · accent `#ebc309`. Every other slug below is
derived from the brand palette; the alert family (success / warning / error
/ info) is retoned to sit on top of paper without hijacking the brand.

| Slug | Hex | Role |
|------|-----|------|
| `base` | `#f3e9cc` | page background (paper) |
| `surface` | `#faf5e8` | cards / panels |
| `subtle` | `#eae0c3` | gentle dividers |
| `contrast` | `#1f5191` | body text (ink) |
| `accent` | `#ebc309` | CTA + brand highlight |
| `accent-soft` | `#f1e1a1` | tinted accent band |
| `primary` | `#ebc309` | resting brand action |
| `primary-hover` | `#b8a62b` | hover state |

**Forbidden uses**:

- Accent applied to decorative elements (borders, dividers, icon fills) —
  accent is reserved for "this is the action".
- Off-palette colors anywhere outside the table above.
- Drop-shadows on anything except active form states.

## Typography

- `display` — Futura (Impact, Arial Narrow, sans-serif)
- `sans` — Futura Book (system-ui, Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif)

**Forbidden**:

- Display font on long-form body copy.
- All-caps body copy.
- Letter-spacing > 0.05em on body text.

## Composition

- **Hero**: neutral
- **Density**: balanced
- **Ornament**: none
- **Borders**: hairline
- **Photography**: neutral

## Required signals

- Borders are 1px hairlines in a chrome tone

## Forbidden signals

- Hard 2px+ borders (reads as poster, breaks editorial register)

## Allowed exceptions

These document deliberate decisions in the shipped bauhaus theme.
The vision reviewer should treat them as intent, not regressions.

- Hero `display` heading on `home` may consume up to 50% of viewport
  height when `composition.hero` is `type-led` or `poster-led`.
- Buttons may be slabs (no border-radius) when `composition.borders` is
  `hard`.
